First test

Data exploration

I got 10 TMS4 sensors (website) from a SoilTemp (website) call. One of the sensors doesn’t work so I did preliminary tests with 9. These sensors are set to sample every 15 min and measures 3 temperatures (soil, ground and 10cm above the ground) and soil moisture:

The idea was to place the sensors in the Botanical Gardens of Cartagena but the day I planned to go, there were road blockages from the national strike. So, I ended up placing them sensors in the gardens of the building I live in. I chose 5 treatments:

Grass (x2)

Bushes (x2)

Wall (shadow) (x2)

Palm undestory (x2)

Air -not buried- (x1)
